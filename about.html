About Our Lab

Advancing cognitive neuroscience through innovative research on memory, attention, and neural mechanisms of human cognition.

Our Mission

To advance our understanding of human cognition through rigorous neuroscientific research, focusing on memory, attention, and cognitive processes. We aim to bridge laboratory findings with real-world applications to improve human cognitive function.

Our interdisciplinary approach combines cutting-edge neuroimaging techniques, behavioral experiments, and computational modeling to uncover the neural mechanisms underlying cognitive processes.

Research Approach

Our lab employs a multi-method approach to investigate cognitive processes, utilizing cutting-edge techniques to understand the neural basis of cognition.

Behavioral Experiments

We design and conduct rigorous behavioral experiments to measure cognitive processes such as memory, attention, and decision-making in controlled laboratory settings.

Neuroimaging Techniques

We utilize advanced neuroimaging methods including fMRI and EEG to observe brain activity during cognitive tasks, providing insights into neural mechanisms.

Computational Modeling

We develop and apply computational models to formalize theories and interpret experimental data, creating a bridge between behavior and neural activity.
