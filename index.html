Our Research Focus Areas

Our interdisciplinary research spans several key areas in cognitive neuroscience, with a focus on understanding the neural basis of memory and cognition.

Memory Processes

Investigating encoding, storage, and retrieval mechanisms in both short-term and long-term memory systems.

Attention & Perception

Exploring how attentional mechanisms influence perception and information processing in the brain.

Emotion & Cognition

Examining how emotional states affect cognitive processes and memory formation across different contexts.
